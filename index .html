About Hosanna wholesalers

Our company's foundation is built on the right product selection, tools, and information.

We drive market innovation through a nimble and insightful approach to business, representing a portfolio of high-quality fast-moving consumer goods.

Mission

To provide quality products and services to our customers through excellent service, careful supplier selection, ethical practices, and continuous investment in human capital and technology in order to exceed the expectations of all stakeholders.

What we do

Sourcing

We find,evaluate and engage suppliers of quality consumer products to achieve cost savings and the best value for goods for our customers. The goods range from sanitary products to consumables from Ugandan manufacturers over to world renowned manufacturers.

Warehousing

From suppliers, we store a fine selection of products with great care not to contaminate any of them. The products are grouped based on their nature and then allocated the necessary storage precautions.

Delivery last-mile

Using a network of vans and expert personnel, we transport products from our stores to your shop without you ever breaking a sweat. Just place an order and we shall it delivered instantly.
